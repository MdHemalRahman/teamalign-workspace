# Overview

EMP Open Source exists to provide operational clarity around employee work without relying on cloud-only services or opaque automation.

## Why EMP exists

- To document work and time consistently
- To reduce ambiguity around task ownership and completion
- To give organizations full control over their employee data

## Scope boundaries

EMP Open Source focuses on recording and structuring information, not real-time communication or automated decision-making.

### Included:

- Time, attendance, and breaks
- Task and work tracking
- Leave and salary-related records
- Company policies and structure

### Excluded by design:

- Multi-tenant architecture
- Third-party messaging or API integrations
- AI-based analytics or monitoring
- Real-time chat or bots
